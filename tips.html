Getting Started

Character Selection

Choosing the Right Character

Selecting the right character for your playstyle is crucial:

  • Chrono is great for aggressive players who need protection
  • Alok is perfect for support players who want to heal teammates
  • Kelly is ideal for players who rely on movement and speed
  • K is excellent for players who want to manage EP and HP effectively
  • Dimitri is great for team players who want to provide healing zones

Pro Tip: Try different characters to find one that matches your playstyle.

Ability Usage

Knowing when and how to use character abilities is key:

  • Use active abilities at the right moment for maximum impact
  • Save abilities for critical situations rather than using them immediately
  • Coordinate ability usage with your teammates for combo effects
  • Be aware of ability cooldowns and plan accordingly
  • Use abilities to counter enemy abilities

Warning: Don't waste your abilities when they're not needed. Save them for when they can make a real difference.

Character Combinations

Some characters work better together than others:

  • Alok + Chrono: Great for aggressive pushes with healing and protection
  • Kelly + Caroline: Excellent for fast-paced gameplay with shotguns
  • K + Kapella: Powerful combination for EP management and healing
  • Dimitri + Moco: Strong for team support with healing and enemy tracking
  • Wukong + Clu: Effective for stealth gameplay with camouflage and enemy detection

Success Tip: Discuss character selection with your team to ensure a balanced composition.

Pet Ability Usage

Understanding how to maximize your pet's abilities:

  • Be aware of pet ability cooldowns and plan accordingly
  • Use pet abilities to complement your character's abilities
  • Some pet abilities are passive and don't require activation
  • Coordinate pet abilities with your teammates for team benefits
  • Upgrade your pets to increase their ability effectiveness

Warning: Don't forget to equip your pet before starting a match!

Pet Upgrades

Upgrading your pets can significantly improve their abilities:

  • Use pet food to level up your pets
  • Higher level pets have stronger abilities
  • Some pets have special abilities that unlock at higher levels
  • Focus on upgrading pets that complement your main character
  • Consider upgrading multiple pets for different situations

Success Tip: A fully upgraded pet can provide significant advantages in combat.

Weapon Attachments

Using the right attachments can significantly improve your weapon's performance:

  • Scopes improve accuracy at different ranges
  • Muzzles reduce recoil and improve accuracy
  • Magazines increase ammo capacity and reload speed
  • Foregrips reduce recoil and improve stability
  • Stocks improve movement while aiming

Warning: Some attachments may not be compatible with all weapons. Check compatibility before equipping.

Weapon Switching

Knowing when to switch weapons is crucial for survival:

  • Switch to your secondary weapon when out of ammo in your primary
  • Use shotguns for close-range combat
  • Use snipers for long-range engagements
  • Switch to pistols when both primary and secondary are empty
  • Consider switching weapons based on the enemy's armor type

Success Tip: Practice quick weapon switching to minimize downtime in combat.

Combat Strategies

Positioning

Good positioning is key to winning gunfights:

  • Use cover to protect yourself from enemy fire
  • Stay mobile to avoid being an easy target
  • Use high ground for better visibility and shooting angles
  • Avoid staying in open areas for too long
  • Use Gloo Walls strategically to create cover

Pro Tip: Always be aware of your surroundings and potential escape routes.

Aiming Techniques

Improving your aim can significantly increase your chances of winning:

  • Practice headshots for maximum damage
  • Use tap firing for better accuracy at medium to long range
  • Control recoil by pulling down while firing
  • Use peek shooting to minimize exposure
  • Pre-aim around corners to be ready for enemies

Warning: Don't spray and pray. Controlled bursts are more effective than continuous fire.

Team Combat

Coordinating with your team during combat is essential:

  • Stick together but maintain spacing to avoid being wiped by grenades
  • Use pincer attacks to flank enemies
  • Coordinate ability usage with your team
  • Call out enemy positions and movements
  • Share resources and ammo with teammates

Success Tip: A well-coordinated team can defeat even the most skilled individual players.

Survival Strategies

Landing Strategy

Choosing the right landing spot can set you up for success:

  • Choose high-loot areas if you're confident in your combat skills
  • Land in less popular areas for a slower start but better survival chances
  • Consider the safe zone location when choosing where to land
  • Land near vehicles for quick rotations
  • Coordinate landing spots with your team

Pro Tip: If you're landing in a popular area, be ready to fight immediately upon landing.

Resource Management

Managing your resources effectively is crucial for survival:

  • Prioritize healing items and armor over ammo
  • Keep a balanced amount of different ammo types
  • Use grenades strategically rather than hoarding them
  • Share resources with teammates who need them
  • Loot eliminated enemies quickly but safely

Warning: Don't carry too much of one ammo type. Balance your inventory for different situations.

Rotation Strategy

Knowing when and how to rotate is essential for survival:

  • Plan your rotations based on the safe zone location
  • Use vehicles for quick and safe rotations
  • Avoid rotating through open areas when possible
  • Rotate early to avoid being caught by the safe zone
  • Use Gloo Walls to create cover during rotations

Success Tip: Good rotation strategy can often be more important than combat skills for reaching the final circles.
